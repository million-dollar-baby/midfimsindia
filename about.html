About

Mindfilms international is one of India's leading full production service Company offering a complete line of service to the local and international entertainment industry. We're passionate about work and aim to provide clients with the best shooting experience possible. It's a place where commercials and feature films are made. Company has a highly flexible approach to production demand, combining hard work and adaptability, a wide range of location in all over India and all over world, coupled with strict budget controls. We facilitate feature films, commercials, documentary, with the aim to shoot anywhere in India. Our creative team has worked on some of the biggest global brands, throughout the world. Our credo is to craft outstanding films with great production values.
India's position gives the opportunity of experiencing 3 seasons in one unique season in various parts of the country, which makes it an ideal place for shooting national and international films.
Our services include obtaining clearances from various authorities.
